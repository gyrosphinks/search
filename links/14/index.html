Why I chose this topic

What I learned


In 2050...

  • The world will have around 9.6 billion people
  • People will live longer so 16 and 1/3 of the people on earth will be over 65
  • "Computers May be 1,000x Times Better - And Much Cheaper"
  • Solar will be used much more because Climate Change will be more prominent
  • We will run low on resources
